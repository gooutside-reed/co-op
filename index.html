Welcome to the Reed Backpack Co-Op!


We are a free gear rental service for Reed College Students, Staff, and Faculty! Due to technical issues, as of Fall 2025, this website is now for the use of Backpack Co-Op managers only. At this time, we are not accepting any reservations or pre-order requests. Come in any time during our open hours to use us as a resource for trip planning, what to bring for a camping trip, or anything else outdoor-related!

Please use our new browser website to explore all the gear that we rent out at the Backpack Co-Op!
