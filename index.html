Welcome to the Reed Backpack Co-Op!


We are a free gear rental service for Reed College Students, Staff, and Faculty! This website allows you to browse all of the gear that we have available to rent. This website is designed to be used either in-person at the Co-Op for the fall semester 2025, no Pre-Orders. Come in any time during our open hours to use us as a resource for trip planning, what to bring for a camping trip, or anything else outdoor-related!

The Backpack Co-Op hours can be found on our website, linked below!
